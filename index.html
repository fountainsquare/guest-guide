Good to Know
Indianapolis Cultural Trail

An 8-mile urban trail that connects Fountain Square to downtown, Mass Ave, and other neighborhoods. Great for walking, running, or biking. You can pick it up right on Virginia Ave — it runs directly through the neighborhood. indyculturaltrail.org

Pacers Bikeshare

Rental bike stations are scattered along the Cultural Trail, including in Fountain Square. Grab one with the app and ride to downtown, Mass Ave, or Broad Ripple. A quick, fun way to get around without a car.

Rideshare & Getting Around

Uber and Lyft both work well in Indy. Downtown is about a 5-minute drive (or a 20-minute walk on the Cultural Trail). Mass Ave is about 10 minutes north.

Groceries & Essentials

The nearest full grocery store is Kroger on South East St (~5 min drive). For quick essentials, there's a CVS on Shelby St just south of Fountain Square.

Nearest Hospital

Community Hospital East — about a 5 minute drive. For urgent care, IU Health is ~10 min south on Shelby St.

Fountain Square Vibe

The neighborhood is walkable, artsy, and safe. Most restaurants and bars are clustered along Virginia Ave and Prospect St. Weekends can get lively — street parking fills up, so arrive early if you're heading out on a Friday or Saturday night.
